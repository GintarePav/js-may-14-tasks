Your Personal To-do List is Here!

  2. You can click on your new task's status to change it to "In progress".
  3. Once you've completed a task, just press on a checkmark next to it, and we'll register it as completed.
  4. If you want to undo the completion of a task, click on the checkmark again. Be sure to review its status afterwards.
